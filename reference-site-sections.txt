SECTIONS FOR YOUR "SEEN AND PAID" LANDING PAGE
(based on the reference site thevisiblemethod.com/home)

Hi Inesa! Below are the sections the reference page has, top to bottom.
Please mark each one YES (I need it) or NO (skip it), and add any notes.

[ ] 1. Hero — big opening headline with your main promise, a "Book your call"
       button, and a "limited spots" note.

[ ] 2. Social proof — your key numbers (followers, views, clients) and/or logos
       of brands you've worked with.

[ ] 3. Problem section — describes the pain your ideal client feels (great at
       what she does, but invisible and underpaid).

[ ] 4. Booking calendar — a calendar embedded right on the page to book a call
       with you (e.g. Calendly).

[ ] 5. Who it's for — 2-3 types of clients (e.g. beginners, coaches, business
       owners), each with their struggle and a call to action.

[ ] 6. Your method — your coaching framework/steps with a memorable name
       (theirs is a 4-week "SEEN" method).

[ ] 7. What's inside — extras that come with the program (resources, community,
       workshops, tools).

[ ] 8. Results showcase — screenshots/numbers proving your own results.

[ ] 9. Program curriculum — what's covered week by week (topics per week).

[ ] 10. What's included — clear list of deliverables (calls, support, materials,
        access after the program).

[ ] 11. Weekly outcomes — what the client walks away with after each week.

[ ] 12. Schedule table — simple table: week / lesson / call topic / homework.

[ ] 13. Success stories — 1-3 short client case studies with real numbers.

[ ] 14. Testimonials — quotes or message screenshots from happy clients.

[ ] 15. About you — your story, photo, credentials, why you do this.

[ ] 16. Community — a private group clients join during/after the program.

[ ] 17. "Is this you?" — who the program IS for and who it's NOT for.

[ ] 18. FAQ — 5-6 common questions answered (time needed, how to apply, etc.).

[ ] 19. How it works — 3 steps to join: apply, book a call, start.

[ ] 20. Final call to action — one last headline + "Book your call" button.
